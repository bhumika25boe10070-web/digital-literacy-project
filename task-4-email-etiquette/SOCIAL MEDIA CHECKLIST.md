&#x20;**SOCIAL MEDIA DO’s \& DON’Ts**



**DO’s**

* Maintain a professional and respectful tone
* Think before posting content
* Use privacy settings wisely
* Verify information before sharing
* Build a positive digital presence



&#x20;**DON’Ts**

* Do not share personal or sensitive information
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not spread fake news or rumors
* Avoid engaging in online arguments
* Do not misuse others’ content without permission
